Our Purpose

Practitioners shaping the next generation of builders

The Build Studio Advisory Council brings together practitioners from business, technology, and entrepreneurship who share our belief that the best way to train the next generation of business+AI builders is to give them real problems to solve.

Council members advise on curriculum direction, sponsor challenges for the course and hackathon, serve as judges and critics at Sprint presentations, and help us build pathways from the classroom to organizations doing meaningful work with AI.

Membership is by invitation. If you're interested in getting involved, we'd love to hear from you.

Membership

What council members do

Members engage with Build Studio in four concrete ways — each one connects industry expertise to student learning.

📋

Curriculum Input

Advise on course structure, challenge intake process, and skill priorities for the B.S. Business+AI program.

🏆

Sponsor Challenges

Submit real business problems for Build Studio teams and hackathon competitors — and see working prototypes built in response.

🎤

Sprint Critiques

Guest-critique student presentations at Sprint 1, Sprint 2, or the Final demo — bringing a practitioner lens to product decisions.

🤝

Student Connections

Open doors to internships, project partnerships, and career pathways for Build Studio students and alumni.

What to Expect

A lightweight, high-impact commitment

Council membership is designed to fit into a practitioner's schedule. We ask for meaningful engagement, not heavy time commitments.
