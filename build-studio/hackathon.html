About the Event

Build with AI. Win with ideas.

The Business+AI Hackathon explores how artificial intelligence can support the development of products and services — apps, tools, and systems — by rapidly generating code. As AI increasingly handles technical implementation, the event emphasizes strong business ideas, creative design, and compelling use cases that create real value for organizations and society.

Teams are given a real-world business challenge in the morning, spend the day building with AI-enabled tools, and pitch their working prototype to industry judges in the afternoon.

For Companies

Partner with us

Company partners submit a real business challenge and send representatives to serve as judges. Here's what we ask:

What We Ask from Company Partners

  1. Provide a business challenge — a short statement describing a real-world problem or opportunity where an AI-enabled tool could create value. Teams will develop both a technical artifact and a business case.

    Example: A retail bank receives thousands of customer inquiries through its chatbot, but the chatbot doesn't always correctly understand customer requests. Design an AI-enabled system that improves how the chatbot interprets queries while generating insights about emerging customer needs. Teams may use datasets like Banking77 (13,000+ queries across 77 banking intents).
  2. Attend as judges — company representatives attend the final presentations and help select winning teams.
⏱ 2-hour commitment · Final session only (3–5 PM)
Submit a Challenge
Challenge Example

What a good challenge looks like

The best challenges are specific enough to guide teams but open enough for creative solutions. They include a real business context, a clear problem statement, and an indication of what success looks like.

Example Challenge

A retail bank receives thousands of customer inquiries through its chatbot about various issues, but the chatbot does not always correctly understand customer requests or generate insights about emerging customer needs. Design an AI-enabled system that improves how the chatbot interprets and responds to customer queries while generating useful insights for the bank.

Teams may use public datasets · Banking77 · Kaggle · Brightdata

Judging Criteria

  • ✓ Strength and originality of the business idea
  • ✓ Quality and functionality of the working prototype
  • ✓ Clarity and persuasiveness of the business case
  • ✓ Effective use of AI in the solution
