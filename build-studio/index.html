BAI 411

Three sprints to a real product

Every team moves through the same three-sprint arc — sketching, validating, and delivering a working AI-enabled product to a real partner organization.

1
Weeks 1–5
Stand Up Your Narrative
User research · System diagram · Lo-fi prototype · 3 user stories
3-min presentation + lo-fi demo
2
Weeks 6–10
Validate Your Narrative
Working prototype · User testing (n=10+) · Data architecture
4-min presentation + working demo
3
Weeks 11–15
Deliver
Feature-complete product · Final validation (n=15+) · Handoff package
6-min final presentation to partner org
